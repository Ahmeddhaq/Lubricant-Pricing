LUBRICANT PRICING & TRADING SAAS – SUMMARY

CORE INTENT:
Build a complete system to manage lubricant blending, pricing, export trading, and sales operations with a strong focus on profitability and real-time decision-making.

KEY BUSINESS PRIORITIES:

* Accurate cost build-up from formulation (base oil + additives)
* Margin-based pricing (not markup)
* Multi-market pricing (GCC, Africa, Asia)
* Multi-pack product handling (1L, 4L, 20L, drums)
* Container-level profitability (20FT / 40FT)
* Freight, logistics, and credit cost integration
* Fast and accurate quotation generation
* Mobile access for sales teams
* Real-time data and system-driven decisions

CORE SYSTEM MODULES:

1. FORMULATION ENGINE

* Recipe-based costing per SKU
* Base oil and additive composition tracking
* Cost per liter and per batch calculation

2. COSTING ENGINE

* Full cost build-up (materials, blending, packaging, logistics, overhead)
* Historical cost tracking

3. PRICING ENGINE

* Margin-based pricing
* Market-based pricing strategies
* Tiered pricing (distributor, retail, bulk)
* Currency conversion

4. SENSITIVITY ENGINE

* Base oil price impact simulation
* Freight and cost fluctuation analysis
* Margin compression visibility

5. TRADING & EXPORT MODULE

* Container optimization (SKU mix, volume planning)
* FOB / CIF pricing calculations
* Country-specific pricing logic
* Credit terms impact (30/60/90 days)

6. SALES SYSTEM

* Customer management (CRM-lite)
* Quote builder (multi-SKU, multi-pack)
* PDF quotation generation
* Deal tracking (open, negotiation, won, lost)
* Mobile app for field sales

7. ANALYTICS & DASHBOARD

* Profit per SKU, container, and market
* Best-performing products and regions
* Margin and loss alerts
* Decision support dashboard

8. ENTERPRISE FEATURES

* User roles and access control
* Approval workflows (pricing, discounts)
* Multi-region and multi-currency support
* Cloud-based scalable infrastructure

9. INTEGRATIONS

* ERP systems
* Accounting tools
* Logistics systems
* Communication tools (email, messaging)

STRATEGIC POSITIONING:
A specialized “Lubricant Trading & Pricing Intelligence Platform” designed for blending and export businesses, focusing on profitability, operational efficiency, and real-time decision support.

END GOAL:
Transform from manual Excel-based pricing into a fully integrated, scalable SaaS platform used daily by finance, sales, and management teams.
